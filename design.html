A little about me

Hi there! That charming guy up there is Rob McDonald. Are you looking for a multidisciplinary thinker? Great. I love helping creative companies with design and code, ranging from MVPs, to supporting large-scale projects such as BBC, Bupa and Lloyds TSB. I aim to combine creativity, technical know-how and a social conscience.

An illustration and graphic design graduate, I made the switch to digital at Th_nk, one of the UKs top digital agencies. Since then, I’ve worked freelance for community-based organisations making a difference. Bringing Art to the masses with Voluntary Arts, giving a helping hand to those in need at NNA, or kickstarting companies with their MVPs.

A point of interest I’m currently studying is datascience, predominantly in practical business intelligence and analytics.

A part-time foodie, drummer and traveller.
