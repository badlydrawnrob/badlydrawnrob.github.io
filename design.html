Counselling Anywhere was an app allowing visitors to search counsellors for Skype therapy sessions. I created wireframes, basic visual design and coded a working MVP. Our aim was to validate the founder's theory that clients needed a better solution than it's competition. The focus was on search, attractive profiles, quality content and ease-of-use for clients.

Many counsellors signed up to the service, but clients didn't use the search function as expected. Tests and adjustments were made, but unfortunately, due to well-funded competition and lack of visitor activity, the project was shelved.

The founder, with a small investment, was able to save time and money validating their idea.
